Privacy Notice
Last updated: June 2026
1. Who we are

Still Here is a pet memorial website that allows people to create and share memories of their departed pets. The website is operated as a personal project.

Contact: stillhere.memorial@gmail.com

2. What information we collect

When you create a memorial on Still Here, we collect the following information:

  • Your pet's name
  • Your pet's species and years of life (optional)
  • A written memory or tribute (optional)
  • A photo of your pet (optional)

We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information about you as the user. Memorials are submitted anonymously.

3. How we use your information

The information you submit is used solely to display your pet's memorial publicly on the Still Here website, and to store the memorial in our database so it remains accessible over time.

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose.

4. Legal basis for processing (UK GDPR)

We process the information you submit on the basis of your consent. By submitting a memorial, you are giving us permission to store and display that content publicly on the website.

5. Who we share your information with

We use the following third-party services to operate the website:

  • Supabase — our database and file storage provider, used to store memorial text and photos. Data is stored on servers in the EU (Ireland).
  • Vercel — our website hosting provider, used to serve the website to visitors.

We do not sell your data to any third party, and we do not share it with advertisers or data brokers.

6. How long we keep your information

Memorials are kept indefinitely unless you request removal. If you would like a memorial to be deleted, please contact us at stillhere.memorial@gmail.com with the name of the pet and we will remove it promptly.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • The right to request deletion of content you have submitted
  • The right to ask what information we hold
  • The right to object to how we use your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact stillhere.memorial@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Still Here does not currently use cookies or tracking technology. If this changes in the future, this notice will be updated accordingly.

9. Children's privacy

Still Here is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has submitted content, please contact us and we will remove it.

10. Changes to this notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.

11. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Notice, please contact us at stillhere.memorial@gmail.com.
